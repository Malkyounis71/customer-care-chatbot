### Common technical issues and solutions

**Issue: Can't log in to my account**
- Reset your password using the "Forgot Password" link
- Clear your browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser
- Contact support if issue persists

**Issue: Product is running slow**
- Check your internet connection
- Close unnecessary applications
- Restart the application
- Check system requirements
- Contact support for optimization

**Issue: Data not syncing**
- Verify internet connection
- Check sync settings in your account
- Force manual sync
- Clear app cache
- Contact support if unresolved

### How do I submit a support ticket?
1. Log in to your account at cobcompany.com
2. Click "Support" in the main menu
3. Select "Submit Ticket"
4. Choose your issue category
5. Provide detailed description
6. Attach screenshots if relevant
7. Click "Submit"

Our team will respond within 24 hours (1 hour for enterprise).

### What information should I provide when contacting support?
To help us assist you faster, please provide:
- Your account email
- Product name and version
- Detailed description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce the problem
- Screenshots or error messages
- Your operating system and browser
- When the issue started
